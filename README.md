# Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales, profit, margins, and growth across segments, products, and countries for 2013–2014. It focuses on pricing impact, product profitability, and monthly performance trends to support data-driven decisions.

# Key Metrics
Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin %
YoY Growth %

# Dashboard Highlights
Sales distribution by segment
Monthly sales and profit trends
Pricing impact: sales vs units sold
Product-level profitability comparison
Profit by country and segment
# Key Insights
High sales volume does not always mean higher profit
Pricing impacts profitability more than volume
Enterprise and Channel Partner segments show stronger margins
Product profitability varies significantly
